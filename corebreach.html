In this game, you control 5 stickfigures and fight in a turn based combat system against enemies and bosses.

I basically made the entire limbus company battling system within 1 semester (this was my game dev high school final semester project)

However, in 2026 (jan), i wanted to revamp it and finally post it to itch.io for people to play.




so far, we have got 50k downloads!! i appreicate it so muche everyone (3/5/26 last updated)
