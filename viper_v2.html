Information

In Unity, for my Game Design III semester final, I updated my aerial combat simulator in which you and your team face against the Viper Squadron.

The player can strafe left and right, spin around, use a gatling gun, missiles, and deploy flares. The AI ships (team and enemy) can use missiles, gatling guns, EMPs, and RAILGUNs.


Viper, the main enemy, can use EMPS, rocket storms, and a railgun, and has significantly higher HP.


To counteract this, your team (including yourself) are given shields that regenerate when you take no damage for a certain amount of time.


Aiming for the player requires you to aim ahead of where the enemy plane is; you need to predict their flight path.


You are able to choose your arsenal with 81 different combinations of attacks and setups!
