FAQ

This is the FAQ page for the UNDERTALE WITHIN YOUTUBE application!

Q) What are the controls?

A) WASD to move your heart, Z to heal, SPACE to activate ability (shield, dash) and Q to hide/show heart.

Q) What heart colors?

A) 1 - red heart - not affected by gravity
2 - blue heart - affected by gravity
3 - orange heart - if you don't move you take damage
4 - yellow heart - SPACE to dash
5 - green heart - hold SPACE to activate shield

Q) I cannot control my heart!

A) Because the application is its own tab, to control the heart you need to first [CLICK] on it, then you can control. Every time you click something else, you need to reclick the heart to control it again.

Q) What is the dynamic border system?

A) If using the dynamic border system, you first need to set the border color to the color of the current bullet box border,
then drag and drop the blue corner points to the bullet box. Finally, enable the dynamic border and it will start to automatically shift the bullet box border for you.

Q) It's a virus!

A) I guarantee you this program I made is not a virus or any type of malware. Most antiviruses / virus checkers assume it is because it uses key logging
(tracks SPECIFIC keys to move the heart, etc.) and screenshots (takes screenshots of your screen to detect damage/border, etc.) for NON MALICIOUS purposes.



Watch out! This program is VERY buggy, so if you encounter any different problems or want to report something, go to the [DISCORD] in the [RESOURCES] section of this page!
