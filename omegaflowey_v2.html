Information

This is the omega flowey boss fight (phase 2) from Undertale.

The player(red circle) has to dodge around flowey's attacks. You can heal via the green boxes and fight by interacting with the orange box.




Flowey has many attacks, including: bombs, vines, guns, time control, chompers, lasers, and a lot more.


Michael's Description

UNDERTALE was also one of the great games I used to play (I'm still interested in it though). However, getting to this fight was a hassle, and you can only play it once

It was too much of a banger to only play once, so i made it in like 12 hours (stunning visuals, i know).
