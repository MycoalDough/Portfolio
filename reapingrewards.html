This game/app won 1st place for the Congressional App Challenge as well as 2nd for FBLA American Enterprise at state level.

It teaches concepts such as: supply and demand, mortgage, stocks, interest rates, certificates of deposit, bank loans, debt, and a lot more.


The player needs to pay their increasing mortgage every 60 days, and needs to use their finance skills in order to keep up with this.


Gameplay-wise, the player can: plant and harvest crops, buy and sell these crops, and deploy robots to help.
