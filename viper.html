Information

In Unity, in 3 days, I created an aerial combat simulator in which you and your team face against the Viper Squadron.

The player can strafe left and right, spin around, use a gatling gun, missiles, and deploy flares. The AI ships (team and enemy) can only either use missiles and use gatling guns.




Viper, the main enemy, can use EMPS, rocket storms, and a railgun, and has significantly higher HP.


To counteract this, your team (including yourself) are given shields that regenerate when you take no damage for a certain amount of time.


Aiming for the player requires you to aim ahead of where the enemy plane is; you need to predict their flight path.
