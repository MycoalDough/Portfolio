Information

This is the very first game in Unity that I finished and released publicly with the intent to play!

This game was made to be played on mobile (Google Play Store) but was also ported to PC via itch.io. It has 17 levels, not including tutorials, special stages, and more!


This game features 20+ enemy types, 20+ team unit types, 19 levels, and 5+ different types of levels (ambush, survive, etc.)!


The goal of the game is to destroy the enemy castle without losing yours. As you progress through the campaign, you earn more and more technology to build your empire!
