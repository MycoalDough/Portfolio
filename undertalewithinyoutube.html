Information

This program creates an UNDERTALE heart on your screen, allowing you to control it like in the actual game. However, you can actually play UNDERTALE in Youtube now.

It contains many settings which people can alter, import, and export for custom levels. It has many custom settings from other UNDERTALE AU's.

This project let me learn how to do object detection (for the bullet box)




Dev Logs have garnered over 100k views and expanded my awesome community greatly. :)
